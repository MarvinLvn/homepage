About me

Hi there ! I am a PhD student based in Paris. My research focuses on learning language from child-like data ! By recording what stimuli children receive (speech, vision, etc.), we can build computational models behaving in a child-like manner. These models can be examined to predict developmental delays and learn more about what is useful for acquiring language. But we can also exhibit failure cases where the child is better than the machine at doing its job in order to try to fix the machine.
